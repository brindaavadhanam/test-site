courses I took:

AP Biology: AP Bio was a super fun experience for me. Traumatizing at times, of course, but which AP isnt? I know a lot of people advise 9th graders to wait til sophomore year until they take APs. In my case, taking AP Bio freshman year only made the jump from 1 AP to 3 APs (in 10th grade) easier.

I'm kinda sad I decided not to go past Spanish 6, but I think I would've gotten ruthlessly bullied in AP Spanish bc of my accent.
